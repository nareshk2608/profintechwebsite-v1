About Us

Profintech is the brain child of a highly accomplished team of technology and finance professionals from across the globe. Backed by a vigorous market research strategy and driven by our team’s highly successful experience in building/scaling large scale FinTech, HealthTech and Financing Businesses, we are building Profintech to make finance both simple and affordable! It is our aspiration that everything we build should stand the test of time, be highly complementary and facilitate further innovation in our ecosystem of trade and finance.

Our ideology:

Profintech was born as a revolutionary idea to help businesses – especially the small businesses to simplify and approach finance as an essential business service. We believe that finance for basic business needs should be available on tap and in a manner that every business irrespective of their size & standing should make it ancillary to their core. Excessive intermediation, complexity and mistrust have traditionally made simple finance inaccessible and expensive for businesses. Several small businesses across the World are challenged every single day with problems of high cost and complex financing frameworks. Driven by our passion to stimulate financial well-being of businesses, we are building technologies & platforms that will promote a world where finance is not only simplified but will also become cost & access efficient.

What are we building?

We are at the drawing board and having fun in building something really cool!

  • We are currently building our tech-stack and platform on the back of a robust market research exercise covering hundreds and thousands of businesses, customer preferences, financing structures, go-to-market models, technology evolutions and innovations that will define the future of finance. We believe that this research will help us build and deliver products that are fresh, innovative and new to market in many different ways!
  • We believe that starting-up new is a great gift that our time has given us. We are grateful for this opportunity and will always be committed to building positive and fantastic products for our customers.
  • Our pledge is to constantly strive to build products that will never harm our environment and will be beneficial for the people, families, society, nation and world at large.
  • Finally, we believe that our company culture and values should always guide us to build the right products that not only serve our customers well but can also evolve beautifully with the time and disruptions happening in our market environment.
